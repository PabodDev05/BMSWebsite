37 Years of Excellence

Boy's Model School, Malabe, nestled in the vibrant locale of Malabe, Sri Lanka, stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development since its inception on May 28, 1987. Founded by W.K. Dep, this esteemed institution has grown remarkably from its modest beginnings, now boasting over 5,000 students guided by a dedicated staff of more than 200 educators. The school offers a comprehensive education from grades 1 to 13, nurturing young minds in a nurturing and dynamic environment, with a commitment to fostering academic prowess, sportsmanship, and moral integrity.
